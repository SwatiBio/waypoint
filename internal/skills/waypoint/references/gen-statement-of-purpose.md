# Statement of Purpose

## Options
- **tone**: Formal | Conversational | Academic | Passionate
- **length**: Short (500) | Medium (1200) | Detailed (2000 chars)
- **focus**: Research | Experience | Goals | Balanced
- **include future plans**: bool

## Structures
- **Formal** — academic background · research experience · goals · why this program · closing
- **Conversational** — hook · journey · what drives you · why here · vision
- **Academic** — field-context · contributions · methodology · program-fit · research-proposal
- **Passionate** — origin-story · pivotal-moment · growth · commitment · impact-statement

## Rules
- Every paragraph ties back to why this program
- No generic filler
- Include specific faculty/program mentions if known

## SOP checklist
- Opens with clear purpose, not a quote
- Shows not tells (specific projects, not "I am passionate")
- Connects past → present → future arc
- Names the program and why it fits
- Closes with intent, not clichés

## Done when
- Follows the tone's structure
- Respects length cap
- Every paragraph connects to the target program
- All checklist items pass
