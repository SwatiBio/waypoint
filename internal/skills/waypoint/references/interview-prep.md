# Interview prep

Question banks + sample answers + research checklist per role.

## Options
- type: Phone Screen|Technical|Behavioral|System Design|Final Round|Full Prep (Full Prep)
- difficulty: Beginner|Intermediate|Advanced (Intermediate)
- # questions: 5|8|10|15 (10)
- sample answers: bool (yes)

Counts by level: Junior 8 · Mid 10 · Senior 12 · Lead 15. Max 5/section.

## Structures
- **phone screen**: self-intro · motivation · background-check · logistics · candidate-Qs
- **technical**: general · technical · behavioral · role-specific · research-checklist · company-specific
- **system design**: requirements · estimation · data-model · api-design · scalability · tradeoffs
- **sample answers**: intro · experience-pitch · challenge-framework (STAR) · skills-to-highlight · Qs-to-ask
- **full prep**: technical + sample answers

## Steps
1. `waypoint jobs get <id>`
2. read profile
3. gen questions for type; add company research checklist (mission, products, stack, culture)
4. if answers on → draft self-intro, STAR pitch, smart Qs to ask
