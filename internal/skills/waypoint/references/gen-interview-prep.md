# Interview Prep

## Options
- **type**: Phone Screen | Technical | Behavioral | System Design | Final Round | Full Prep
- **difficulty**: Beginner | Intermediate | Advanced
- **questions**: 5 | 8 | 10 | 15 (scales with level: Junior 8, Mid 10, Senior 12, Lead 15)
- **sample answers**: bool

Max 5 questions per section.

## Structures
- **phone screen**: self-intro · motivation · background-check · logistics · candidate-Qs
- **technical**: general · technical · behavioral · role-specific · research-checklist · company-specific
- **system design**: requirements · estimation · data-model · api-design · scalability · tradeoffs
- **sample answers**: intro · experience-pitch · challenge-framework (STAR) · skills-to-highlight · Qs-to-ask
- **full prep**: technical + sample answers

## Done when
- Every section has the target question count
- Research checklist includes company-specific items (mission, products, stack, culture)
- STAR answers reference real experience from profile
